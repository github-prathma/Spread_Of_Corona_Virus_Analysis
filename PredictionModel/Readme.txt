This proediction is about creating a predict model based on the confirmed cases
with certain symptoms, the prediction is based on the age group, country, sex,
and several symptoms, where I have categorized into cold, cough, fever, symptoms.
And with whether has direct or indirect relation with Wuhan, this model predict 
whether this case would be recover or tend to have danger of death.
This prediction is used for decision process for the hospital, like when the 
supplement is in a shortage, this predict can help the hospital to arrange the 
space and machines accordingly.
